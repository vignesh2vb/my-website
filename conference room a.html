Conference Room A

Conference Room A is a modern, fully-equipped space for IQAC meetings and workshops. With state-of-the-art facilities and comfortable seating, this room is perfect for discussions, presentations, and group activities.

Features:

  • Seating capacity: 50 people
  • High-speed internet and Wi-Fi access
  • Projector and sound system
  • Whiteboard and markers
  • Comfortable ergonomic chairs
  • Air conditioning

Location:

123 University Building, Ground Floor, City Campus.

Availability:

Available from Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
