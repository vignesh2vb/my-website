What is IQAC?

The Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) is a body established by educational institutions to ensure continuous improvement in the quality of education, research, and institutional operations.

Mission

Our mission is to promote a culture of quality by engaging in continuous monitoring, feedback, and improvement practices that benefit both academic and administrative activities.

Key Initiatives

  • Curriculum Development and Innovation
  • Student Feedback and Performance Enhancement
  • Faculty Development Programs
  • Quality Audits and Accreditation

Our Vision

We aim to be a leading institution for quality in education, recognized for our commitment to excellence, innovation, and student success.
