Auditorium

State-of-the-Art Auditorium

Our auditorium is designed for large conferences, presentations, and cultural events, providing a professional and comfortable environment for all attendees. It features high-end audio-visual systems, ergonomic seating, and ample space for diverse events.

Key Features:

  • Seating Capacity: 300 people
  • High-definition projector and sound system
  • Stage lighting and microphone setup
  • Wi-Fi access for all attendees
  • Air conditioning
  • Handicap accessible

Location & Booking:

The auditorium is located at the University Campus, Block B. Available for events Monday through Friday, from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM.
