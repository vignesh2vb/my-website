About IQAC

Internal Quality Assurance Cell

Our Mission

IQAC aims to build and promote a culture of quality, transparency, and excellence in all academic and administrative endeavors. We focus on continuous improvement through monitoring, assessment, and feedback mechanisms that drive institutional growth.

Our Objectives

  • Quality Enhancement: To continuously improve the academic and administrative performance of the institution.
  • Holistic Development: To promote a learner-centric environment that fosters personal and professional growth.
  • Feedback Mechanism: To collect and act upon feedback from various stakeholders, including students, parents, faculty, and management.
  • Institutional Accountability: To maintain transparency and ensure accountability in all institutional processes.
  • Benchmarking and Best Practices: To develop and apply benchmarks for academic excellence and implement best practices across the institution.

Functions of IQAC

  • Development and application of quality benchmarks.
  • Promoting innovative teaching and learning methods.
  • Organizing workshops, seminars, and conferences on quality-related themes.
  • Documenting various activities that lead to quality improvement.
  • Coordinating quality-related activities within the institution.
  • Preparing and submitting the Annual Quality Assurance Report (AQAR).
